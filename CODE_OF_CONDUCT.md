# Code of Conduct

This project is for practical, respectful collaboration around AI-agent workflows.

## Expected Behavior

- Be direct and constructive.
- Critique ideas and changes, not people.
- Keep examples free of private data.
- Assume maintainers may reject scope expansion to keep the skill focused.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment or personal attacks.
- Publishing secrets, tokens, private repository details, or personal data.
- Spam, low-effort promotion, or unrelated marketing requests.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove issues and pull requests that violate this policy.
